Our Story 🌿

FreshMart started in 2018 as a small neighbourhood store with a simple mission: bring the freshest produce and daily essentials directly to the people of Koramangala at honest prices.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted local brand serving thousands of happy families daily — without losing the warmth and personal touch of a small-town shop.

We partner with local farmers and trusted suppliers to ensure every product on our shelf meets our high quality standards.
